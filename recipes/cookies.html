Cookies Recipe

Description

Cookies are so comforting and convivial, and they are also easy to make, that's why I love them!

This recipe is for about 12 cookies.

Warning: This recipe is created for the purpose of a web development exercise, I don't recommend following it.

Ingredients

  • 180g of flour
  • 6g of baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 60g of unsalted butter
  • 100g of brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 7.5g of vanilla sugar
  • 100g of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the over to 180Â°C
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, the baking soda and the salt
  3. Add the brown sugar, the softened butter, vanilla sugar and egg
  4. Once everything is well combined, add the chocolate chips
  5. Make small balls with the dough and put them on a plate with baking paper
  6. Cook them for 12 minutes
