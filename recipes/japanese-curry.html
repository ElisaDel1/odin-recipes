Japanese Curry Recipe

Description

Japanese curry is an easy recipe that many people in Japan do. When living there, I was myself making it a lot!

This dish contains a protein part (it can be chicken, beef, tofu, or anything you like), some vegetables (usually potatoes and carrots), and it is accompanied with rice or noodles. The curry itself is made with an already made curry paste, and water!

Warning: This recipe is created for the purpose of a web development exercise, I don't recommend following it.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Rice
  • Japanese Curry (e.g. Golden Curry)

Steps

  1. Cut the onions in dices chunks, and the garlic in small chunks. Add them in a large pot with some olive oil.
  2. Cut the vegetables in big chunks and add them to the pot.
  3. Cut the chicken is size-bites chunks and add them to the pot.
  4. Cook some rice aside.
  5. Add some boiling water to level in the pot. Then let it boil for a few minutes until everything is almost cooked.
  6. Add the curry and let it simmer.
  7. When every is cooked, place the rice on the half of a plate, and the curry with a lot of sauce in the other part.
  8. Enjoy!
